Chicken

Description

This is a homemade chicken dish originated right from the rivers of the bayou. This will have your mouth tingling for the next bite!

Ingredients

  • Raw Chicken
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Season chicken with pepper, garlic, and salt.
  2. Set oven to 450 F
  3. Put seasoned chicken in oven for 45 minutes or until golden brown
  4. Half way through, toss yah parsley in that bad boy and call it a day
  5. Serve
