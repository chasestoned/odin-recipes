Mac N Cheese

Description

This is a delicious dish made by the cheese for the cheese lovers! Can't beat this classic!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Fill pot with water and boil on 350
  2. Drop dat pasta in and let it sit
  3. Mix cheese in once pasta starts to get done
  4. Finish cooking, season, then serve
